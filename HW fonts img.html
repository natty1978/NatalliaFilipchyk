Seed phrase – Your seed phrase is a list of words that can be used to recover your wallet should you
forget your password or lose access to your wallet. When you first begin trading with your wallet, find
your seed phrase and back it up somewhere safe, in multiple locations if possible.

Dont store your seed phrase on an online cloud storage
service and never share it with anyone.
